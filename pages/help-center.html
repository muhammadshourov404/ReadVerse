How can we help you?

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I publish my first article?
To publish an article, log in to your account and navigate to the 'Write' page from the top menu or your dashboard. Use our distraction-free editor to write your content, add formatting, and click the 'Publish Now' button when you're ready.
Is ReadVerse completely free to use?
Yes! Creating an account, reading articles, and publishing your own stories are completely free. We may introduce premium features in the future, but the core reading and writing experience will always remain free.
How do I change my profile information?
You can update your profile information, including your name, bio, and preferences, by going to the 'Settings' page from your dashboard menu. Don't forget to click 'Save Changes' after updating your details.
Can I delete an article after publishing?
Currently, you can manage your articles from the dashboard. The delete feature is being rolled out in our next update. For immediate removal, please contact our support team.
